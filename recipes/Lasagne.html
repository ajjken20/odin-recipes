Lasagne

Description

A basic recipe of Homemade Lasagne

Ingredients

Basic Bolognese Sauce

  • 1 ibsp of Olive Oil
  • 1 Onion Finely chopped
  • 2 Celery stalks, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • i kg beef mince
  • Half cup (110g) tomato paste
  • 3 quarter cup (185ml) stock or red wine
  • 1 x 400g can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 x 700g bottle of tomatoe passata

Bechamel Sauce

  • 1 L (4cups) milk
  • 1 Brown onion halved and coarsely chopped
  • 8 Fresh parsley stalks
  • 8 Whole back peppercorns
  • 4 Whole cloves
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • 3 tbsps (60g) Butter
  • A third of a cup (50g) plain flour
  • 1 Cup (100g) Parmesan cheese
  • Pinch ground nutmeg, Salt and Pepper to taste

Lasagne

  • Olive Oil extra to grease
  • 5 Fresh lasagne sheets
  • 1 Cup (90g) Mozzarella Cheese
  • Mixed salad leaves to serve

Directions

  1. To make the Bolognese, heat the oil in a large heavy based saucepan over a medium heat. Add the onion, celery and garlic and cook, stirring for 5 minutes or until the onion softens. Add mince and cook, stirring to break up lumps for 5 minutes or until browned.
  2. Add the tomatoe paste and stock or red wine to mince mixture and bring to the boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes or until stock and wine reduces slightly.
  3. Add the diced tomato and passata and simmer, stirring occasionally for 45 minutes or until sauce thickens.
  4. Meanwhile to make the Bechamel sauce, combine the milk, onion, parsleystalks, peppercorns, cloves and bay leaves in a medium saucepan and bring to a simmer over a medium heat. Remove from heat and set aside for 15 inutes to infuse, Strain the milk mixture through a fine sieve into a large jug. Discard solids.
  5. Melt the butter in a large sauepan over a medium heat until foaming. Add the flour and cook, stirring, for 1-2 minutes or until mixture bubbles and begins to come away from the side of the pan. Remove from heat.
  6. Add half of the milk mixture, whisking continuously. Place pan back on medium heat. Whisk until thick add the rest of the milk, whisking continuously. Bring the sauce to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 5 minutes, using a wooden spoon or saptula to stir continuously
  7. Add Parmesan and stir until cheese melts. Taste and season with nutmeg, salt and pepper as desired.
  8. Preheat oven to 180c brush a rectangular 3L (12 cup capacity) ovenproof dish with oil to lightly grease.
  9. Spread a quarter of the Bechamel over the base of the prepared dish. Arrange lasagne sheet over the sauce. Top with a third of the bolognese and a third of the remaing Bechamel. Continue layering with the remaining lasagne sheets, bolonese and bechamel, finishing with a layer of bachamel. Sprinkle with mozzarella.
  10. Bake in preheated over for 40 minutes or until cheese melts, is golden brown and edges are bubbling. Remove from oven and set aside for at least 15 minutes to set.
